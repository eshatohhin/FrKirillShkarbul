During the joyful Christmas season, we must guard against spiritual relaxation and remain vigilant. The core message is that sin lives within each of us, acting as an agent of deception. Merely calling ourselves Christian is not enough; we must actively crucify this sin daily so that Christ may truly live in us. Without this, we risk being swept into the world's delusions, regardless of our preparedness or knowledge.

Four essential points are emphasized for our daily spiritual life: First, recognize and fight the sin within yourself. Second, discern the deception operating in the world and avoid being enslaved by any secular agenda. Third, constantly seek Christ, His truth, and His kingdom as your highest goal. Fourth, strengthen your faith and become a missionary, offering a true testimony to those hungry for God.

Our primary goal must be to become children of God. Everything else loses meaning if we lose Christ and our salvation. Let us spend this holy time wisely, setting spiritual goals and separating ourselves from a world that celebrates sin.
