Christ is risen! During these 40 days after His Resurrection, the Lord appeared to His disciples to prove He was truly alive in His body and to teach them about the coming Kingdom of God—the age to come after the general resurrection. This isn't the end of the world, but the end of an age.

Like Thomas, we may have doubts and need our own experience of the Lord's care. The Gospels don't detail all Christ taught after rising, focusing instead on His earthly life and mission. This is intentional. God created this world with purpose; our calling isn't to escape it, but to live in it—overcoming temptation, loving God and neighbor, and sharing the joy of Christ's truth. Seeing hearts transformed by His love is a great joy.

The world will be renewed. We will have resurrected bodies, living in holiness with God, as angels do. While details are mysterious, Scripture and spiritual life assure us this is real. These 40 days remind us of Christ's closeness. That experience empowers our daily testimony. Many paths lead astray, but the truth shines for those who humbly seek it. May the risen Christ be with you, filling you with His grace and joy as you bear witness to Him. Amen.
