Christ is risen. The key issue is losing a living connection with Christ, which leads to book-worship, like Protestants with *sola scriptura* or Catholics with their catechisms. Even in the Orthodox Church, we can become idolaters of texts—Scripture, Holy Fathers, canons—treating them as a dead manual. But the Word of God isn't frozen; it's alive, applied daily through spiritual life, prayer, and effort. Modern education and laziness kill this: we memorize without understanding, expect a free lunch, and avoid real struggle. We must seek the Holy Spirit's breath in the texts, not just dogmas. Otherwise, we're lukewarm, and Christ will spit us out. True peace comes from zeal, fighting for truth, and living in Him daily. May we gain the joy of His resurrection and be faithful servants.
