God is a mystery who reveals Himself as He chooses. To approach Him, we must learn His language—not logic or philosophy, but the language of typology, the mystical link that connects the Old Testament prophets and patriarchs to Christ. This typology continues in the New Testament, where we become living images of Christ through grace. We must reject the world's images—seen in our gadgets, media, and vanity—and instead cultivate a typological mindset, allowing Christ to be manifested in our hearts, actions, and even our appearance. True faith unites internal devotion with external practice, as seen in the liturgy and sacraments. Let us cleanse our hearts and become true witnesses, so that through us, others may see God.
