We are entering Great Lent, a time for spiritual warfare, not just dietary rules. We live in an age of great deception, where even active church members can drift from truth, as history shows with gradual heresies. To prepare, avoid two wrong approaches: complacent ignorance or paranoid focus on earthly signs like technology or politics. True salvation comes from overthrowing the "earthly spirit" within us—our obsession with careers, hobbies, even external church activities—and undergoing a radical, inner transformation. Our goal is to become spiritual, to love Christ deeply, and to belong to His kingdom, not this world. This Lent, let us break free from earthly attachments and addictions, seek healing in Christ, and strive for true spiritual freedom.
